How will purchasing Enterprise Email benefit me?

Purchasing an Enterprise Email package lets you take advantage of our advanced email technology, giving you least latency along with the industry's best uptime, scalability and reliability. Being served out of the cloud also means no IT hardware, software, bandwidth or people costs, which allows for a simple pay-as-you-grow model.

What are the features that Enterprise Email can provide over Personal Email?

features essential for business communication such as umpteen storage, shared calendaring, global contacts, shared document management, shared task management, push synchronization for mobile devices, MS Outlook & Mac OSX are some of the features provided by Enterprise Email that are not available in Personal Email.

What Email Clients and protocols are supported?

You can send and receive emails using any desktop-based email client such as Microsoft Outlook, Outlook Express, Mozilla Thunderbird, Eudora, Entourage 2004, Windows Mail, etc. We also have a guide on how you can configure different email clients to send/receive emails. The enterprise email product supports the POP, IMAP and MAPI protocols.

How do I use my Webmail Interface?

Its easy! Simply go to http://webmail.yourdomainname.com . Once on the login page, you would need to login with your email address and the corresponding password.

Which mobile phones can I access my mail from?

Our fluidic webmail, built on HTML 5 & Javascript, is compatible on all major Operating systems such as iOS, Android, Windows Mobile, Symbian and Blackberry. Your will be abble to access your email from and any Smartphone or Tablet.

How much storage space will I get provided per Email Account?

Our enterprise email comes with a abundance of storage space. Each mailbox comes with 10 GB space dedicated to email and 2 GB space dedicated to file storage, giving you a toatl of 12 GB of storage per account.

What ports do I need to use for Email Hosting?

Usually, the port used for the Outgoing Mail Server/SMTP Service is 25. However, there might be a situation where your ISP might be blocking the use of port 25 for SMTP service. To circumvent this you can use an alternate port 587 for sending mails.

Can I create mailing lists?

Absolutely! You can create mailing lists, add/delete users, select a moderator, restrict and ban people from joining a list and much more. All of this information is available in our knowledgebase here.

What is your SPAM policy?

We take a zero tolerance stance against the sending of unsolicited e-mail, bulk emailing, and spam. 'Safe lists', purchased lists, and selling of lists will be treated as spam. Any user who sends out spam will have their account terminated with or without notice.

What are auto-responders and can I use them?

Yes, you can. An auto-responder is a program that, when setup for your email address, sends out an automatic pre-set reply to an email, as soon as it is received at this email address. To set up an Auto Responder, please refer to the following KnowledgeBase article.
